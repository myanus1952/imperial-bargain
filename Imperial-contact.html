A few quick answers

When do I get paid? The same day you accept an offer — cash or check, your choice. There's no waiting on a buyer or a payout hold.
How do you decide what to offer? We look at condition, demand, and what similar items have recently sold for, then send a straightforward number — no lowballing, no back-and-forth games.
Do I need an appointment? No. Send photos and a description any time and we'll get back to you — usually within a day. Larger items or estate lots may need a quick in-person look.
